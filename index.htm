About Me

My name is Lawrence Shah and I am a professional web developer based in the Bay Area in sunny California. I'm always looking for opportunities to create awesome things!

Front-end development is where I live and breathe, but I am also available for your full stack needs. I am proficient with PHP and MySQL for back-ends. Whether you need a responsive single-page web app, a smooth store-front site, or a cool personal website, I want to help you make it happen.

In my spare time, I enjoy working on personal code projects and following up on the latest technologies and scientific developments around the web and the world. I'm currently exploring possibilities with the MEAN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, Angular.js, Node.js).
